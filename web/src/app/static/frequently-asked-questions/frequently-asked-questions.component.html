Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Tazaker?

Tazaker is a secure platform for buying and reselling tickets for various events. We aim to ensure safe and transparent transactions for all users.

2. How do I sell a ticket?

To sell a ticket, register with your email and PayPal account, list your ticket for resale, and once itâ€™s purchased, the funds will be transferred to your PayPal account after fees.

3. How does the payment process work?

We use PayPal to handle all payments securely. When a buyer purchases a ticket, the payment is processed via PayPal and funds are sent to the seller after deducting applicable fees.

4. What fees do you charge?

Tazaker charges a small platform fee, along with standard PayPal transaction fees. These fees are automatically deducted from the seller's payout.

5. Can I cancel or refund my ticket?

No, once a sale is completed, Tazaker has a strict no-refund policy. Both buyers and sellers are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of the ticket information.

6. How do I verify if a ticket is valid?

In case of disputes, Tazaker may work with event organizers to verify the validity of tickets. Always ensure that the information provided is accurate to avoid issues.

7. How do I resolve disputes?

If you encounter an issue with your transaction, contact us at info@tazaker.org. We will work with you to resolve the matter and may verify the ticket with the event organizer.

8. How do I delete my account?

You can request the deletion of your account by sending us an email at info@tazaker.org.

9. Is my data secure?

We take your privacy seriously. We only collect your email and PayPal information, which is stored securely. Sessions are protected with JWT (JSON Web Tokens) and managed securely in our infrastructure.

10. Do I need to verify my identity?

At this moment, sellers do not need to verify their identity, but this may change in the future to enhance security and trust on the platform.
